### Recall Questions

#### Context Questions
- Where were you?
- What were you doing there?
- Who else was with you?
- What time of day or year was this?

#### Sensory Questions
- What do you remember seeing?
- What sounds do you hear in your memory?
- Was there a smell associated with this?
- What did you physically feel?

#### Narrative Questions
- What happened just before?
- What did you do next?
- How did it end?
- What happened immediately after?

#### Emotional Questions
- How did you feel?
- Why do you think this mattered?
- Has your perspective on this changed?

#### Probing Follow-ups
- "Tell me more about..."
- "What stands out most about..."
- "Can you describe that moment in more detail?"
- "What do you remember feeling in your body?"
