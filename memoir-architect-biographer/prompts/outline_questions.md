### Outline Questions

#### Scope Questions
1. What period matters most to your story?
2. Where does it begin and end?
3. What is the central experience you want to share?
4. What did you learn or how did you change?

#### Theme Questions
5. What recurring patterns appear in your memories?
6. What values or beliefs guided your choices?
7. What moments define who you became?

#### Structure Questions
8. What is the natural sequence of events?
9. Which moments deserve full chapters vs. mention?
10. What needs to be explained vs. shown?

#### Validation Questions
11. Does each chapter have a clear focus?
12. Are there gaps in the timeline?
13. Does the emotional arc feel complete?
